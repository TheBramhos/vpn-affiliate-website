About CyberGhost VPN

CyberGhost VPN is a mixed breed company. Its software is built in Germany – the place where the company was born. However, for data security reasons, its headquarters are now in Bucharest, Romania.

The reason behind the switch is simple. Romania is one of the most privacy-friendly locations in Europe. Back in 2009, Romania’s Constitutional Court was the first institution to oppose the EU’s Data Retention Directive. And in 2014, it declared the infamous ‘Big Brother law’ unconstitutional.
That’s a big deal because it allows CyberGhost VPN to stay true to its No Log policy. This VPN keeps ZERO logs on your browsing history, traffic destination, data content, or search preferences. For more information, check out CyberGhost’s Transparency Report.
