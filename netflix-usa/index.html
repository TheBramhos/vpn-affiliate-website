Within minutes you can open a whole new Netflix world. This is how to get access to American Netflix.

In the US they are lucky that Netflix has a lot more films and TV shows, compared to most other countries. But you don’t have to use your hard-earned money to take a trip to the US every time you want to stream something. You only need your regular Netflix account and a VPN.

Use a VPN

When you are connected to an American VPN server, it looks like you are in the US, and you can enjoy all the content that is restricted to American people. All VPN’s can do the basic trick, but some of them are better with Netflix than others.

If you connect to an American server you can enjoy the same content as the Americans, if you connect to a British server, you will get the same content as the Brits and so on.

You can also use A VPN to access the free iPlayer from the BBC. Read how.

The Netflix superpower

The United States of America is a superpower in economics, military, culture, and has far superior Netflix content.

American Netflix is booming with titles, that people in other parts of the world don’t have. The reason is rights. When Netflix negotiates rights for series and movies, geography is a crucial component.

The more restricted the geography, the cheaper rights. And being an American company, its not weird that the US Netflix has most titles.
