How to Access PVPro if it’s Blocked in Your Region

By vpnXpert Last Updated: 10 June, 2021

  • How to unblock and access PVPro – step-by-step

Counter-Strike: Global Offensive (CS:GO) is a team-based multiplayer shooter that dominates the global eSports competitions. PVPro is an online community and trading platform that allows you to play CS:GO, participate in tournaments, and gain new ranks and positions on the competitive leaderboards.

PVPro hosts their own servers, where you can earn coins that can be used to open cases with new skins, buy specific skins for your weapons or gear, or even let you buy entry to leagues and tournaments, where you can compete and win even more coins.

It’s completely free to play, all you need to do is set up an account and set up a VPN.

So Why Would You Need a VPN to Access PVPro?

PVPro isn’t necessarily accessible in all countries and regions. 

Although CS:GO can be played on most computers and operating systems, the trading platform in itself can be interpreted as a form of digital gambling. This means that “skin betting” or “skin gambling” without a gambling license is illegal in some countries, such as Norway and Denmark.

Since most of the sites operate internationally, they’ve rarely applied for a gambling license from the various nations’ gambling commissions or authorities.

However, using a VPN you can still access sites that have been geoblocked in your location.

How to connect to PVPro using a VPN

Firstly, you need a VPN. 

At VPNxpert we regularly compare, test, and review the best VPNs available. You can read our reviews here.

Once you’ve chosen your preferred VPN, you need to set up your service to access the site. Most VPN-providers maintain and host a variety of servers and networks, you can funnel your traffic through. That means you can unblock the site, regardless of what country you’re currently in.

If you’re considering playing CS:GO, we recommend you pick a server in a location you’re closest to, geographically. The further away the server is, the slower your connection – and the higher your ping. That can be a life-or-digital-death difference when you’re playing competitively online. 

If able, we recommend you play without VPN, but use the VPN to enter the site.

How to unblock and access PVPro – step-by-step

  1. Sign up for your chosen VPN
  2. Download the app or install the service on your chosen device
  3. Open the app or programme and log in
  4. Choose your server
  5. Once connected, visit PVPro.com

Warning: using a VPN to buy games on Steam from regions with lower pricing is a violation of the terms and conditions you signed when you made your account on Steam. This is despite the fact that geoblocking purchases of digital services is potentially a violation of European Antitrust laws. 

Until Valve chooses to adhere to the EU legislation, you can still be banned and lose access to your Steam account, if you try to circumvent the geoblocking to buy games from a different region. However, playing the games or playing on different servers is not restricted.

Using a VPN Can Wreak Havoc on Your Ping

It’s important to note that your connection can be slowed down if you play CS:GO using a VPN.

VPNs work by funneling your data and traffic through a secure server. That added step is needed for security and to hide your geographical location, but it can also increase your ping. If you’re a seasoned CS-player, you know that a high ping can cause lag.

We recommend that you play without a VPN, if able.
