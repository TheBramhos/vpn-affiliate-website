Surfshark VPN

Surfshark is a new and hot player in the VPN market. Overall, it can be said that Surfshark offers great value for the money you pay. The provider has numerous extra privacy features, high-speed servers, and gives the user the ability to use a single account on an unlimited number of devices. The price is slightly higher than what is set by other VPN providers, yet, the company deserves credits as it tries to improve transparency.

The VPN service is excellent to subscribe to if you are a fan of streaming services and want to get access to, for example, the American version of Netflix. You can use the Surfshark VPN and access the Netflix library in more than 15 different countries.

Surfshark’s servers offer high speed. Fast and reliable servers are of high value when it comes to choosing a VPN service – and we can promise that Surfshark will not disappoint you regarding this. Moreover, the customer service is great and you can live chat with their support 24/7 in several languages.

Surfshark puts its customers’ privacy at the center of their business model. However, it is based on the premise that you must trust Surfshark as a company, which has been questioned by some VPN experts. Surfshark claims that they are not logging their customers’ IP addresses, browsing history, amount of bandwidth used, and so forth. Simultaneously, the privacy policy is low on details and could be further improved. It should be said that overall, Surfshark is trying to be as transparent as possible with protecting the privacy of its customers (which is not necessarily the case with other companies), however, it can still be improved.

In contrast to its competitors, Surfshark is unique as the company allows its customers to use their service on an unlimited number of devices within one account. That to be said, the monthly price of the service is within the range of expensive VPN providers and thus in line with more well-known VPN companies such as ExpressVPN and NordVPN. If you sign up for a monthly subscription the price is approximately 12 US dollars. Longer subscriptions are possible and can make the monthly price decrease to around 2 dollars per month (if you sign up for two years).

If you are unsure whether you like Surfshark’s service, they offer, similar to other providers, a 30 day-back money-back guarantee. This allows you to get a “free account” for 30 days as you can decide to stop the subscription after a month if you are not enjoying their service. In addition, if you download Surfshark’s application on your iOS, macOS or Android, you can enjoy a week-long trial period for free.

Surfshark offers several unique features that are rarely seen among other VPN providers. For instance, one such feature is Surfshark’s Whitelister. This feature is a split-tunneling tool that enables you to access sites that otherwise can be blocked from VPNs. Another advantageous feature is Multihop. The feature Multihop allows you to surf in an even more secure way as this tool creates a VPN connection to a server and then bounces your traffic to a second VPN server.

The overall review of Surfshark is that it is a VPN provider that offers everything you need – and even a little bit more.
